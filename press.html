About Funfinity Games

Funfinity Games is an AI-first indie game studio dedicated to crafting endlessly compelling idle experiences. Founded by a team of veteran game developers, the studio leverages cutting-edge AI to accelerate development while maintaining human creativity at the helm. With a lean team of six, Funfinity is proving that exceptional games don't require massive budgets or bloated dev teams—they require vision, taste, and the right partnership with artificial intelligence.

About Battle Toys

Battle Toys: Idle Tower Defense is an AI-first tower defense game where childhood toys wage epic battles across your home. Command rubber ducks, wind-up robots, dinosaurs, and piggy banks to defend your bedroom, backyard, garage, and beyond. With deep upgrade systems, collectible cards, boss encounters, and beautiful painterly art, Battle Toys offers the strategic depth tower defense fans crave wrapped in a charming, cozy aesthetic that appeals to players of all ages. Launching globally in August 2026 with soft launch in April 2026.
