About Funfinity Games

Three passionate creators using AI to build the next generation of idle games.

"To craft endlessly compelling idle experiences that are easy to start, satisfying to master and rewarding for players"

Funfinity Games was founded by a team of seasoned game developers who believe the future of indie gaming lies at the intersection of human creativity and artificial intelligence. We started with a simple observation: building exceptional games shouldn't require massive teams or crushing budgets. With the right tools and vision, a small team can punch far above its weight.

Our mission guides everything we do. Every feature we add, every asset we create, and every decision we make comes down to one question: Is this endlessly compelling? We obsess over making games that capture attention at first glance, reward mastery, and keep players genuinely engaged for the long haul.

Battle Toys is our first title. It's the culmination of months of focused development, countless iterations, and a relentless commitment to quality. But it's just the beginning. We're building a studio that proves compelling games don't need bloated dev teams—they need vision, taste, and the right partnership with AI.

Our Approach

The AI-First Approach

We don't use AI as a shortcut—we use it as a force multiplier. Artificial intelligence handles the repetitive, time-consuming work while our team focuses on creativity, taste, and vision. The result? Exceptional games built lean, fast, and at a fraction of traditional dev costs.

Our AI-first pipeline reduces traditional development man-hours by up to 92%. That doesn't mean less quality—it means more time for iteration, refinement, and the human touches that make games feel alive.
