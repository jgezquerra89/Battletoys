Privacy Policy

How Funfinity Games handles your data

Effective Date: March 30, 2026

Funfinity ("we," "us," or "our") developed Battle Toys as a free-to-play mobile game available on iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our game.

By downloading or playing Battle Toys, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the game.

1. Information We Collect

Gameplay Analytics. We collect anonymous gameplay data to improve the game experience. This may include:

  • Session duration and frequency
  • Waves completed, upgrades used, and in-game progression
  • Device type, operating system version, and screen resolution
  • Country or region (based on device settings, not precise location)
  • Crash logs and performance data

What We Do Not Collect. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, photos, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account to play Battle Toys.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the anonymous analytics data described above to:

  • Understand how players interact with the game
  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • Balance gameplay and improve game design
  • Make informed decisions about future updates and features

3. Third-Party Services

Battle Toys may use third-party analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics or similar tools) to process the anonymous data described above. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them:

  • Google Privacy Policy (Firebase)

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Advertising

Battle Toys does not currently display third-party advertisements. If we introduce ads in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and we will notify users within the game.

5. In-App Purchases

Battle Toys may offer in-app purchases. All payment transactions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's or Google's privacy policies for details on how they handle payment data.

6. Children's Privacy

Battle Toys is designed for a general audience and is not specifically targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained only as long as it is useful for the purposes described above. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to delete upon request.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data we collect. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including the right to access, correct, or delete personal information. Since Battle Toys does not collect personal information, these rights generally do not apply. If you have questions or concerns, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Press page.
