Features

Why Players Love Battle Toys

🧸

Toy-Themed Battlefields

Watch rubber ducks, dinosaurs, robots, and piggy banks clash in delightfully rendered domestic environments—the living room, kitchen, bedroom, and beyond.

⚙️

Deep Upgrade System

Hundreds of meaningful upgrades let you customize your army. Every choice matters as you min-max your defense strategy to perfection.

🎴

Collectible Cards

Hunt down rare and epic cards to strengthen your towers. Build the perfect team through strategic collection and clever deck composition.

👹

Epic Boss Fights

Every ten waves, face off against powerful bosses that demand your best strategy. Thrilling, tense, and incredibly rewarding to conquer.

⏱️

Idle Progression

Your toys keep fighting even while you’re away. Earn rewards passively and come back to exciting surprises. Gaming on your own terms.

🎨

Painterly Art Style

Hand-painted environments and charming character design create a cozy, inviting world. Beautiful enough to show off, fun enough to play for hours.

About the Studio

Building Games with AI at the Core

Funfinity is a three-person AI-first game studio. We leverage cutting-edge AI to accelerate development while keeping human creativity at the helm. Every game we build is obsessively crafted to be endlessly compelling—easy to start, satisfying to master, and genuinely rewarding.

We believe the future of indie game development is collaborative: humans bringing vision, taste, and soul; AI handling the grunt work and amplifying our capabilities. By working smarter, not harder, we can punch far above our weight and compete with studios 10x our size.
